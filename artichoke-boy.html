Office Life Captain's Log
In the summer of 2020, I was hired on to storyboard, animate, and edit the short film “Artichoke Boy,” written and directed by Phil Eichinger.
The film uses a 2D, crayon-drawn style based on initial character designs by Phil, all brought to life through traditional cel animation.
  • Finalist Love International Film Festival
  • Finalist Humboldt International Film Festival
  • Official Selection VOB Film Festival
  • Official Selection Garden State Film Festival

This young man rocks.
